About Diana.

Diana (19) is a multidisciplinary artist based in Oslo. Working across painting, drawing, and experimental techniques, her practice explores emotion through color, atmosphere, and intuition.

Inspired by music, mood, and personal experience, she moves fluidly between mediums such as acrylics and graffiti, allowing each work to evolve organically.

Rather than offering fixed meanings, Diana creates open emotional spaces that invite viewers to respond through their own feelings, memories, and interpretations.
